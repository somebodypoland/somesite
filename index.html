Key Concepts

Accumulation Trading Method
A systematic approach to building positions in global equities through phased accumulation, risk-managed entries, and disciplined exit strategies.
Setup
A defined trading opportunity within a stock's observation phase, with planned entry points and allocation targets.
Serial
An individual position within a setup, representing actual capital deployed at a specific price level.
Phase
The current stage of a stock in the watchlist lifecycle: Radar, Lokalizacja, Setup, Obserwacja.
